Who We Are

United Civil Group is a leading civil contracting company specialising in a diverse range of earthworks and civil construction services. With a proud history spanning more than three decades, we have established ourselves as trusted partners for projects of all scales and complexities.

Our expertise extends across contaminated site remediation, industrial and urban subdivisions, civil infrastructure upgrades, and comprehensive utility services.

Our Approach

At United Civil Group, we understand that every project presents unique challenges. Our approach combines proven methodologies with innovative solutions, ensuring we deliver outcomes that exceed expectations whilst maintaining strict adherence to timelines and budgets.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, consultants, and stakeholders through open communication and transparency.

Site Remediation

We specialise in the remediation of contaminated sites, working closely with environmental consultants and regulatory bodies to ensure safe, compliant outcomes.

  • Contaminated soil excavation and disposal
  • Groundwater management
  • Asbestos removal and disposal
  • Site validation support
  • Environmental monitoring coordination

Urban & Industrial Subdivisions

Complete subdivision solutions for both urban residential and industrial developments with seamless integration of all civil and utility components.

  • Bulk earthworks and site preparation
  • Road construction and pavement works
  • Stormwater drainage systems
  • Water and sewer reticulation
  • Electrical and communications conduits

Civil Infrastructure

From new installations to upgrades and renewals, our infrastructure capabilities cover all aspects of civil construction built to last.

  • Pavement construction and rehabilitation
  • Retaining wall construction
  • Water main installation and upgrades
  • Sewer main installation and upgrades
  • Bridge and culvert works

Bulk Earthworks

  • Final trim and grading
